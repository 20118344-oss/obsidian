Got questions?

Frequently Asked

How do I book an appointment? +

Fill out the booking form on this page or contact us via Instagram. We'll get back to you within 24 hours to confirm your appointment and discuss your design.

How much does a tattoo cost? +

Pricing depends on the size, placement, and complexity of the design. Small pieces start from €80. We provide a full quote after your consultation. Custom designs are priced individually.

Do I need to prepare anything before my session? +

Eat a good meal before your appointment, stay hydrated, and wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the area being tattooed. Avoid alcohol for 24 hours prior.

What styles do you specialise in? +

Our artists specialise in blackwork, Japanese, dark realism, fine line and geometric tattoos. Each artist has their own style — check their portfolios on Instagram to find your match.

How do I take care of my tattoo after the session? +

We recommend using YAYO Tattoo Butter for aftercare. Keep the tattoo clean, moisturised, and out of direct sunlight for the first 2–3 weeks. Full aftercare instructions are provided after your session.
